Our Donate Plan

Provide support to underprivileged section of the society specially extreme poor/ homeless and orphan children through quality education ,nourishment & shelter to make them resources for the nation.

Food for a family

Food is the most important thing for living. With this help we can provide the flood affected people with food and medicine.We want to inspire a nation with food. Best relation can be created through sharing food.

Shelter for a family

Many of the affected lost their homes and a place to live. We can build or help them build their new homes and get a place to live. The families that walk through our doors are faced with one of the scariest situations any family can imagine: they no longer have a place to call home.

Education for a child

To become a national treasure by offering free education to children and orphans.Many of the students lost their books and study equipments. We can provide them study materials and help them study and get educated.

Their lives depend on your donation

Their lives depend on us now, but we depend on you. Without donations for food, our volunteer cannot do their job, and these helpless animals are left with nothing.
